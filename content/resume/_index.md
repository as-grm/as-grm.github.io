# Aleksander Sandro GRM, PhD
**Assistant Professor of Marine Hydrodynamics & Applied Mechanics**

---

### Academic & Research Profile
Assistant Professor of Marine Hydrodynamics and Applied Mechanics at the **University of Ljubljana**. My expertise bridges the gap between theoretical continuum physics, advanced numerical schemes for partial differential equations (PDEs), and high-fidelity industrial engineering software deployment. 

With an extensive history leading private R&D projects alongside core university lecturing, my work optimizes performance matrices for aerospace propulsion, physical oceanography assets, biomedical treatment simulations, and custom maritime/naval architectures.
